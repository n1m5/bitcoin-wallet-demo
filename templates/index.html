Transaction journey

Payment broadcast
What just happened on the Bitcoin network
Your order total was calculated (10k sats × quantity). The exact amount is sent on-chain.
Your wallet’s UTXOs were selected to cover the snacks + miner fee.
A SegWit transaction was built and signed with your private key (on the server for this demo). The key never leaves your control in a real wallet.
The raw transaction was broadcast to the Bitcoin testnet peer-to-peer network and entered the mempool.
Miners pick up the transaction. Once included in a block it receives its first confirmation. More blocks = more confirmations.
